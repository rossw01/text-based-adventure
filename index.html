Welcome!
Here are some of the commands available to you duing the game:

north, south, east, west, upstairs, downstairs, get, examine, throw, talk, speak, open, use.

You can also add a word after some of the commands.
For example, you can use 'examine itemname' at any point to an item. Or just use "examine" to examine the room.
Give 'examine apple' a try!
